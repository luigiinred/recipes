Apple Crisp

Back
  1. Slice Apples and mix with salt and water in a greased 9x9 pan
    • 4C sliced Apples
    • 1/2tsp Salt
    • 1/4 C Water
  2. Top with Red Hots
    • 1/2C Red Hots
  3. Mix together in separate bowl then sprinkle on top of apple mixture
    • 3/4C Flour
    • 1C Sugar
    • 1/3C softened Butter

Bake at 350 for 45 minutes
Best served warm
